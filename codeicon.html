The Origin of Codeicon


Day 1

INAUGURATION OF CODEICON 2.0


CODEICON 2.0, was the mega event of ICON, a hackathon arranged in order to create a great opportunity to enhance the participant’s skills and earn a huge cash prize at the same time! That, too, in association with General Mills, among the world's larger food companies
CODEICON 2.0 was inaugurated in the presence of honourable chief guest, Mr. Arijit Mukherjee, Head of Information Technology at General Mills.
Mr. Mukherjee addressed the gathering with his encouraging words and spoke about the college, the journey of General Mills in India and about the fight to come out of the COVID times.




Day 2

The second day of the event started with the one of the most highlighted event of ICON 2021-22 Hackathon which was sponsored by “General Mills”.

The hackathon is inter college competition where one team from each college participated and compete with each other in technical skills.
The winning team was from KJ Somaiya who won the cash prize of Rs. 10000 and RunnerUp team was from Gurukul College won cash prize of Rs. 5000 sponsored by “General Mills”.

Simulatenously the event was followed by Gaming event and quizes. This year faculties too participated in quiz session which made it much more exicting. The event ended with by announcing the prizes to the winners and felicitating them by our delegates.
